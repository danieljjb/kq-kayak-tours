About Us

In operation since 2011, Kilmore Quay Kayak Tours offer their customers a fantastic opportunity to explore one of Irelands hidden gems. A family run business where Tom, the owner, has created a unique kayaking experience for all to enjoy. Tom has over 25 years of kayaking experience and is incredibly knowledgable about the local area. Wheter it's fishing off of St. Patricks bridge, kayaking along the coastline or exploring the Saltee Islands, you'll be sure to have an unforgettable experience with Kilmore Quay Kayak Tours.

Tours

Package One

This tour begins from the small beach in Kilmore Quay. From here we will make our way across to the Saltee Islands, about 4km from the starting point. There are a lot of wildlife such as seals and otters to be seen out here. We will explore the island before returning to shore. Kayak, wet suit and life jacket are provided. Duration: Six hours. Lunch not included. NOT FOR BEGINNERS.

Price: €45/pp

Package Two

This tour begins from the small beach in Kilmore Quay. Following this, you will be trained on how to use the fishing rod correctly from the kayak. We will then make our way to St. Patricks bridge, about 1.5km from the starting point. We will spend the rest of the time here fishing. Kayak, wet suit, life jacket and fishing rod are provided. Duration: Three hours. Lunch not included.

Price: €35/pp

Package Three

This tour begins from the small beach in Kilmore Quay. From here we will explore the marina and the coast line in Kilmore Quay. Lots of fish, jellyfish and birds can be seen. Perfect for children. Kayak, wet suit and life jacket are provided. Duration: Two hours. Lunch not included.

Price: €20/pp
